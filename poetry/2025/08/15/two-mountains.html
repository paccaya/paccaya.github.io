Two Mountains

By Ryan Chan

15 Aug 2025 05:50 PM

On the steeped edges of an abyss,
birthed a gaping crevice

Cavernous sides bespoke a tale of rifting connection.
A divide so vast that the binding crust
was forgotten,
yet a river valley began anew.

It is where we seem most divided
that a deep connection can be unleashed.
